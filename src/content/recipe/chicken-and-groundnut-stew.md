---
name: Chicken and Groundnut Stew
author: Molly Smillie
servingsNumber: 9
prepTime: 15 minutes
ingredients: 
    - 9 chicken thighs
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt
    - 2 medium onions, chopped
    - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper, or more
    - 2 cloves garlic
    - 2 bay leaves
    - 2 teaspoons olive oil
    - 2 cups chicken broth
    - 1 teaspoon curry powder
    - 1 can tomato sauce, 15 oz can
    - 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
    - 3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
instructions:
    - Brown thighs in olive oil. Set aside.
    - Saute onions until lightly browned. Add garlic, curry, thyme, salt and cayenne and stir for one minute.
comments: We like it served over rice. Try it, you'll like it!
nutritionFacts: 447.4 Calories
category: Entrees
---
